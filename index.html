Hi! Welcome to my MScBMC Blog for the MSC2006H Media Design Course.

01 My MRP

I am creating a 2D/3D animation on navigating women's sexual health after radiation cancer treatment for a virtual sexual health clinic. The animation focuses on what radiation is, what effects it can have on your body and how that impacts your sexual health, and what management strategies you can do. The style is minimalistic and warm, with focus on engaging animation transitions and patient inclusivity.

02 What I Want to Learn

I am new in the interactive world, so my goal is to learn the basics of website building, familarize myself with the basic language and get an introduction of coding. Since my MRP is part of the animation stream, I was maybe thinking of creating a version of my animation that is a scrolly telly, where users can click for additional information at certain parts. I also want to learn how to intergrate a 3D asset and videos into a website.
